# Feedback Rules

## How to Give Feedback

After any build, tell me what you think. I'll update the library.

### Examples

**Like something:**
- "That hero worked great"
- "Love the coastal blue for this garage company"
- "The 3-card services section was perfect"

**Dislike something:**
- "Hated the wave divider on that garage site"
- "The serif font felt wrong for HVAC"
- "That dark theme was too heavy for a dentist"

**Context-specific feedback:**
- "Use wave dividers only for coastal/pool businesses"
- "Serif fonts are perfect for law, avoid for trades"
- "Gradient overlays work when there's strong photography"

### What I Do With It

1. Update `library/taste-profile.md`
2. Adjust pattern/aesthetic ratings
3. Refine reasoning engine recommendations
4. Apply to future builds automatically
