# Coastal Blue Theme

**File:** `library/themes/coastal-blue.md`

---

## Source
Coastal Garage Doors — coastal-door-flow.base44.app

---

## Best For
- Coastal location businesses
- Home services (garage doors, landscaping, pool services)
- San Diego, Florida, coastal California markets
- Light, approachable, trustworthy vibe

---

## Vibe
Light, breezy, trustworthy. Coastal blues evoke the ocean and sky without being literal. Clean and residential-friendly. Professional but not stuffy.

---

## Color Palette

**Primary Background:** White
- `#FFFFFF` - Clean, bright

**Secondary Background:** Soft coastal blues
- `#E8F4F8` - Very light blue for sections
- `#D0E8F2` - Slightly deeper for contrast sections

**Primary Color:** Deep coastal blue
- `#1E5F8E` - Headers, primary text
- `#2A7CB5` - Lighter blue for accents

**Accent Color:** Ocean blue
- `#4A90B8` - CTAs, highlights
- `#5BA3C8` - Hover states

**Text Colors:**
- `#1E5F8E` - Primary text (deep blue)
- `#5A6B7A` - Secondary/muted text
- `#FFFFFF` - Text on dark backgrounds

---

## Visual Treatment

**Backgrounds:**
- Predominantly white
- Soft blue sections for visual rhythm
- Wave/shape dividers between sections
- Gradient fades between color sections

**Typography:**
- Clean sans-serif
- Friendly, approachable weight
- Clear hierarchy

**CTAs:**
- Ocean blue buttons
- White text on blue
- Consistent throughout

**Dividers:**
- Wave shapes at section boundaries
- Soft curves (not sharp angles)
- Can be solid color or gradient

---

## When to Use
- Coastal location businesses
- San Diego, Florida, coastal California
- Home services (garage doors, landscaping, pools)
- Residential-focused services
- Want approachable, not corporate
- Light, airy aesthetic desired

---

## When NOT to Use
- Urgent/emergency services (too calm)
- Dark/luxury positioning
- Urban/industrial aesthetic
- Conservative professional services (law, finance)
- Winter/cold climate markets
