# Industrial Bold

## Best For
- Construction
- Trades
- Manufacturing
- Automotive
- Industrial services

## Vibe
Rugged, professional, no-nonsense. Strong typography, bold colors, straightforward layouts.

## Color Palette
- Background: `#FFFFFF` or `#F5F5F5`
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Primary: `#374151` (charcoal)
- Accent: `#EA580C` (burnt orange) or `#FACC15` (construction yellow)
- Text: `#111827`

## Typography
- Font: `Oswald`, `Bebas Neue`, `Roboto Condensed`
- Bold, condensed headlines
- Clean sans body

## Components
- Sharp or slightly rounded corners: `4px`
- Bold buttons
- Strong contrast
- Industrial imagery

## When to Use
- Construction
- Trades
- Manufacturing
- Auto repair
- Equipment rental

## When NOT to Use
- Beauty/wellness
- Luxury
- Children
- Creative agencies
