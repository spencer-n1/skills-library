# Taste Profile

Spencer's accumulated design preferences based on feedback.

---

## Always

- Real photos over stock when possible
- 100vh heroes for main landing pages
- Navy blue for trust-based businesses (legal, financial)
- Clean solid backgrounds (patterns optional)
- Maximum 3 service cards on homepage
- High contrast text (4.5:1 minimum)

---

## Never

- Purple gradients on white (generic AI look)
- Wave dividers for trades/garage businesses (feels mismatched)
- Inter font as primary (overused)
- More than 2 font families per site
- Light gray text on white (poor readability)

---

## Context-Dependent

### Serif Fonts
- **Use for:** Law, luxury, editorial
- **Avoid for:** Tech, emergency services, older demographics

### Dark Themes
- **Use for:** Emergency services, tech/SaaS
- **Avoid for:** Healthcare, coastal businesses, wellness

### Gradient Overlays
- **Use for:** Premium positioning, strong photography
- **Avoid for:** Budget/concrete contractors, fast conversion needed
