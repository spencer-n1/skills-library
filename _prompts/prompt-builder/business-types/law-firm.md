# Business Type: Law Firm / Attorney

**Category:** Professional Services
**Typical Client:** Solo practitioners, small firms, specialized attorneys
**Primary Goal:** Lead generation (consultations)

---

## Recommended Pattern Stack

### Navigation
**Primary:** `centered-logo-curved-tagline.md`
- Creates established, trustworthy feel
- Business name is brand
- Professional but not stuffy

**Alternative:** Standard split nav for very modern firms

### Hero
**Option A:** `full-clean-image.md`
- Professional office or courthouse imagery
- Clean, premium feel
- Best for: Established firms, premium positioning

**Option B:** `gradient-text-dark.md`
- Modern, bold headline
- Good for: Personal injury, younger firms, aggressive positioning

### Sections (in order)

1. **Why Choose Us** (`why-choose-us-professional.md`)
   - Experience, credentials, results
   - Professional photo of attorney

2. **Services** (`services-3-card.md`)
   - 3 main practice areas
   - Examples: Personal Injury, Family Law, Criminal Defense

3. **Process** (`process-numbered-cards.md`)
   - 4 steps: Consultation → Strategy → Execution → Resolution
   - Builds confidence in process

4. **Reviews** (`reviews-carousel.md`)
   - Client testimonials
   - Critical for trust

5. **Stats** (`stats-bar.md`) — Optional
   - Years of experience, cases won, etc.
   - Place early in page, not at bottom

### CTA
**Final Banner** (`final-banner.md`)
- "Schedule Your Free Consultation"
- Phone number prominent

### Footer
**Standard Multi-Column** (`standard-multi-column.md`)
- All pages linked
- Contact info
- Privacy policy (important for legal)

## Design Direction

**Colors:**
- Navy (#1a1a2e, #16213e)
- Charcoal (#0f0f0f, #1a1a1a)
- Gold accents (#c9a961, #d4af37) for premium feel
- Or: Deep green (#1e3a2f) for trust

**Typography:**
- Serif for headlines (authority): Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond, Merriweather
- Sans-serif for body (readability): Inter, Source Sans Pro

**Imagery:**
- Professional headshots
- Courthouse exteriors
- Office interiors (clean, modern)
- Books/libraries (sparingly)

**Feel:**
- Authoritative but approachable
- Established but not outdated
- Trustworthy above all

---

## Content Guidelines

**Headlines:**
- Focus on outcomes, not features
- "Get the Justice You Deserve" not "We Practice Law"
- "Protect Your Family's Future" not "Family Law Services"

**Trust Signals:**
- Bar associations
- Years of experience
- Case results (where allowed)
- Client reviews
- Free consultation

**Avoid:**
- Aggressive sales language
- Guarantees of outcomes
- Stock photos of random gavels/scales
- Too much legal jargon

## Prompt Assembly Notes

When building for law firm:
1. Always mention "professional photography" not "stock images"
2. Emphasize trust and authority in tone
3. Include multiple CTAs for consultation
4. Privacy policy page is required
5. Mobile must have click-to-call
