# Competitor & Alternative Pages

Create competitor comparison and alternative pages that rank for competitive search terms.

## When to Use
- Building comparison pages for SEO
- Creating battle cards for sales
- Positioning against specific competitors
- Capturing "alternative to [competitor]" search traffic

## Don't Use When
- You don't have clear differentiators
- You're not ready to be honest about competitor strengths

## Core Principles

1. **Honesty Builds Trust** — Acknowledge competitor strengths
2. **Depth Over Surface** — Go beyond feature checklists
3. **Help Them Decide** — Be clear about who each option is best for
4. **Modular Content Architecture** — Centralize competitor data for reuse

## Page Formats

### Format 1: [Competitor] Alternative (Singular)
**URL:** `/alternatives/[competitor]`  
**Target:** "[Competitor] alternative"

**Structure:**
1. Why people look for alternatives
2. Summary: You as the alternative
3. Detailed comparison
4. Who should switch (and who shouldn't)
5. Migration path
6. Social proof from switchers
7. CTA

### Format 2: [Competitor] Alternatives (Plural)
**URL:** `/alternatives/[competitor]-alternatives`  
**Target:** "[Competitor] alternatives"

**Structure:**
1. Why people look for alternatives
2. What to look for in an alternative
3. List of alternatives (include real options)
4. Comparison table
5. Detailed breakdown of each
6. Recommendation by use case
7. CTA

### Format 3: You vs [Competitor]
**URL:** `/vs/[competitor]`  
**Target:** "[You] vs [Competitor]"

**Structure:**
1. TL;DR summary
2. At-a-glance comparison table
3. Detailed comparison by category
4. Who you're best for
5. Who competitor is best for (honest)
6. Customer testimonials
7. Migration support
8. CTA

## Research Process

For each competitor, gather:
1. **Product research** — Sign up, use it, document features/UX
2. **Pricing research** — Current pricing, what's included
3. **Review mining** — G2, Capterra, TrustRadius for themes
4. **Customer feedback** — Talk to switchers
5. **Content research** — Their positioning, comparison pages
