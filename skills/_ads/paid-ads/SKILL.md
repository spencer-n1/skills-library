# Paid Ads

Plan, launch, and optimize paid advertising campaigns that drive profitable customer acquisition.

## When to Use
- Scaling customer acquisition
- Testing messaging and offers
- Reaching new audiences
- Retargeting website visitors
- Promoting content or events

## Don't Use When
- You don't have conversion tracking set up
- Unit economics don't support paid acquisition
- You're looking for organic growth only
- Landing pages aren't optimized

## Platform Selection

### Google Ads (Search)
**Best for:** Intent-based, bottom-funnel, high intent keywords

**Campaign Types:**
- **Search:** Text ads on Google search
- **Performance Max:** AI-driven across all Google properties
- **Display:** Banner ads on websites
- **YouTube:** Video ads

**When to use:**
- People search for your solution
- High commercial intent keywords
- Local services (Google Local Services Ads)

### Meta (Facebook/Instagram)
**Best for:** Interest targeting, visual products, B2C

**Campaign Types:**
- **Awareness:** Reach, brand awareness
- **Traffic:** Website clicks
- **Engagement:** Post engagement, page likes
- **Leads:** Lead forms, conversions
- **Sales:** Conversions, catalog sales

**When to use:**
- Visual products/services
- Interest/behavior targeting
- Lookalike audiences
- Retargeting

### LinkedIn Ads
**Best for:** B2B, professional targeting, high ACV

**Campaign Types:**
- **Sponsored Content:** Native posts in feed
- **Sponsored Messaging:** InMail, Conversation Ads
- **Lead Gen Forms:** Pre-filled forms
- **Text/Dynamic Ads:** Sidebar ads

**When to use:**
- B2B with high customer value
- Job title/company targeting
- ABM (account-based marketing)

### Other Platforms
- **TikTok:** Younger audiences, creative brands
- **Twitter/X:** Real-time, tech, thought leadership
- **Pinterest:** Visual products, DIY, fashion
- **Reddit:** Niche communities, authentic approach
- **Programmatic:** Display, video at scale

## Campaign Structure

### Account Hierarchy
```
Account
├── Campaign (budget, goal, targeting)
│   ├── Ad Set (audience, placement, bid)
│   │   ├── Ad (creative, copy, CTA)
│   │   └── Ad
│   └── Ad Set
└── Campaign
```

### Best Practices
- One objective per campaign
- 3-5 ad sets per campaign (testing)
- 2-4 ads per ad set (creative testing)
- Naming convention: [Objective]_[Audience]_[Date]

## Campaign Setup Process

### 1. Goal Definition
- Primary KPI (conversions, leads, traffic, awareness)
- Target CPA or ROAS
- Budget and timeline

### 2. Audience Definition

**Targeting Types:**
- **Interests:** Topics, behaviors, demographics
- **Custom Audiences:** Email lists, website visitors, app users
- **Lookalike Audiences:** Similar to your best customers
- **Retargeting:** Website visitors, video viewers, engagers

### 3. Creative Development
- Multiple angles/hooks
- Various formats (image, video, carousel)
- Strong CTAs
- Platform-native look and feel

### 4. Tracking Setup
- Pixel installation (Meta, LinkedIn, etc.)
- Conversion events defined
- UTM parameters for Google Analytics
- Attribution model selection

### 5. Launch & Monitor
- Start with 3-5 ad sets
- Daily budget: $20-50 per ad set minimum
- Check daily for first week
- Kill underperformers quickly

## Optimization Framework

### Week 1: Learning Phase
- Don't make major changes
- Let algorithms gather data
- Kill ads with <1% CTR (or high CPC)
- Watch for tracking issues

### Week 2-4: Optimization
**If CTR is low:**
- Test new creative angles
- Refine targeting
- Check ad frequency (fatigue)

**If conversion rate is low:**
- Landing page optimization
- Offer/messaging mismatch
- Audience quality

**If CPA is too high:**
- Lower bids/budgets
- Narrow targeting
- Improve conversion rate
- Test new offers

### Ongoing: Scale Winners
**Horizontal Scaling:**
- Duplicate winning ad sets to new audiences
- Test new interests/lookalikes
- Expand geographic targeting

**Vertical Scaling:**
- Increase budget 20% every 3-5 days (if profitable)
- Don't exceed 30% increase per day

## Key Metrics

**Awareness:**
- CPM (cost per 1000 impressions)
- Reach, Frequency
- Video view rate

**Engagement:**
- CTR (click-through rate)
- CPC (cost per click)
- Engagement rate

**Conversion:**
- CPA (cost per acquisition)
- ROAS (return on ad spend)
- Conversion rate
- Quality score/relevance score

## Budget Allocation

**Testing Budget:**
- 20% of total budget
- New audiences, creative, offers

**Proven Budget:**
- 80% of total budget
- Scaled winning campaigns
