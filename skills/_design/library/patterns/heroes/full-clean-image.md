# Hero Pattern: Full Clean Image Background

**Source:** Julian Vance Law Offices (prime-legal-grid.base44.app) + Summit HVAC feedback
**Best for:** Professional services, luxury brands, portfolio sites, when image is premium
**Vibe:** Premium, clean, confident (not desperate)

## Description
Full-width hero section with clean, high-quality background image. Minimal overlay, centered or left-aligned content. No contact form in hero — lets the image and strong CTAs do the work.

## Layout Structure
```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                             │
│     [Clean, full-bleed background image]    │
│                                             │
│     [Headline]                              │
│     [Subheadline]                           │
│     [CTA Buttons]                           │
│                                             │
│     (NO form — image is the focus)          │
│                                             │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## Visual Specs

**Background Image:**
- Full coverage, no repeating
- High quality, professional photography
- Subject matter relevant to business
- Light or dark depending on text color needs
- Minimal to NO overlay (or very subtle dark/light wash)

**Content:**
- Headline: Large, bold, constrained max-width
- Subheadline: Smaller, supportive text
- CTA buttons: Primary + secondary (NO form)
- Position: Centered or left-aligned (not scattered)

**Typography:**
- Headline: Bold sans-serif or appropriate serif
- Must contrast strongly with background
- Generous line-height

**Height:**
- 80-100vh (full viewport feel)
- Or large but not full (600-800px)

## When to Use
- Law firms (courthouse, professional imagery)
- Luxury services (high-end photography)
- Portfolio showcases (work speaks for itself)
- HVAC/home services with premium truck/team photos
- When you have EXCELLENT imagery
- When you want confident, not desperate, feel

## When NOT to Use
- Poor imagery available
- Need immediate lead capture (use form instead)
- Emergency services where phone must be prominent

## Variations
- **Centered content:** All elements centered
- **Left-aligned:** Content anchored left
- **With stats bar below:** Immediate credibility after hero
- **Dark image:** White text
- **Light image:** Dark text

## Prompt Snippet
```
Hero Section: Full clean image background (NO form)
- Background: Full-width, high-quality image of {IMAGE_SUBJECT}
- Overlay: Minimal or none — image should be clean and visible
- Height: 85vh or approximately 700px
- Content position: {CENTERED / LEFT_ALIGNED}
- Headline: "{HEADLINE_TEXT}" — {FONT_SIZE}, {FONT_WEIGHT}, {COLOR}
- Subheadline: "{SUBHEADLINE_TEXT}" — smaller, {COLOR}
- CTAs:
  * Primary: "{PRIMARY_CTA}" — {BUTTON_STYLE}
  * Secondary: "{SECONDARY_CTA}" — outline or text style
- NO contact form in hero (let image breathe, use floating CTA or form below)
- Typography must contrast clearly with background
- NO heavy gradient overlays that obscure image
```

## Notes
- Image quality is EVERYTHING here — bad image ruins the whole site
- Clean doesn't mean boring — the image should have visual interest
- Text must be readable without fighting the background
- NO form in hero creates confident, non-desperate feel
- Use floating chat CTA or contact form in dedicated section instead
