# Navigation Pattern: Centered Logo with Curved Tagline

**Source:** Julian Vance Law Offices (prime-legal-grid.base44.app)
**Best for:** Professional services, law firms, premium brands
**Vibe:** Authority, trust, established

## Description
Centered logo with primary business name above, curved/tagline text below. Menu items split evenly on left and right sides. Clean serif typography for authority.

## Layout Structure
```
[Menu Left] ──── [LOGO] ──── [Menu Right]
                  ↓
            curved tagline
```

## Visual Specs

**Logo Area:**
- Centered horizontally
- Primary name: Serif font (authority/trust)
- Tagline/business type below: Smaller, curved text (arc effect)
- Example: "JULIAN VANCE" / "LAW OFFICES" (curved)

**Menu Left:**
- HOME | WHY US | OUR WORK | REVIEWS
- Uppercase, spaced tracking
- Clean sans-serif or matching serif

**Menu Right:**
- SERVICES | PORTFOLIO | Phone | CTA Button
- Phone: Icon + number
- CTA: Contrasting button (blue on light bg, for example)

**Behavior:**
- Sticky on scroll
- No slot machine animation (static, professional)
- Optional: subtle background blur on scroll

## When to Use
- Law firms, attorneys, financial advisors
- Any business wanting established/trustworthy feel
- When business name is important brand element
- Premium service businesses

## Variations
- **Dark nav:** White text on dark background (high contrast)
- **Light nav:** Dark text on light/off-white background
- **With phone:** Prominent phone number for emergency services
- **Without phone:** Clean, minimal for non-urgent businesses

## Notes
- The curved tagline is the signature element — makes it feel custom/luxury
- Serif font choice critical for legal/financial trust signals
- Balance is key — equal visual weight on left/right menus
