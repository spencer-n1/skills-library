# Voice Message Templates

---

## Main Template

**Use this as the base for most business types:**

> "Hey [name], thanks for replying! Real talk — I build websites for {business_type}s. I noticed you don't have a site and since you said {hook_reference}, figured you'd want people to actually find you online. I actually put together a concept for you already. No pressure — just 10 minutes to check it out. What do you think?"

---

## Variations by Tone

### Casual / Relaxed

> "Thanks for getting back to me! So here's the thing — I'm a web designer and I've been helping {business_type}s get more clients. I noticed you don't have a website and I actually built one for you already just to show what it could look like. Would you be down to take a quick peek? Takes like 5 minutes and if you hate it, no worries at all."

### Direct / No BS

> "Hey appreciate you responding. Full transparency — I build websites for businesses like yours. I noticed you either don't have one or it could use work, so I went ahead and made you one. Just wanted to see if you'd be interested in seeing it. Free to look, no commitment. What do you say?"

### Local Connection

> "Cool, thanks for the reply! So I'll be straight with you — I'm a web designer here in {area} and I help local {business_type}s get found online. I built a site concept for your business and wanted to see if you'd be down to check it out. Just 10 minutes, you can tell me if it's trash or not. Interested?"

---

## Hook Reference Phrases

Use these to connect the voice message back to the hook:

| Hook Type | Reference Phrase |
|-----------|------------------|
| Location scope | "since you said you work all over {area}" |
| Client types | "since you said you take all types of clients" |
| Service range | "since you do both [X] and [Y]" |
| Add-on services | "since you offer [service]" |
| Availability | "since you're available for [emergency/weekends]" |

---

## Alternative Closes

**Soft close:**
> "...would it be terrible to take a quick look?"

**Curiosity close:**
> "...figured I'd show you since I already did the work. What do you think?"

**Direct close:**
> "...you free for like 10 minutes this week?"

**Low pressure:**
> "...no pressure at all, just figured I'd ask."

## If They Have a Website Already

> "Hey thanks for replying! Real talk — I build websites for {business_type}s. I came across your site and noticed it could use some improvements, so I actually built you an upgraded version. Since you said {hook_reference}, figured you'd want more people finding you. Would you be open to a quick look? Just 10 minutes."

---

## Follow-Up Voice (If They Don't Reply to First Voice)

> "Hey [name], just following up on my last message. No pressure at all — I know you're busy. Just wanted to see if you had any interest in seeing what I built. If not, totally cool. Either way, hope you're having a good week!"

---

## The Portfolio Build (Discounted Offer)

> "Hey [name], thanks for replying! So I'm a web designer and I saw your profile — noticed you don't have a website yet [or: noticed your site could use some work]. Since I'm building my portfolio right now, I'm doing heavily discounted pricing — no big upfront cost, only the monthly hosting fee. I already put together a website for you. Would you have 5-10 minutes to check it out? If you like it, I'll publish it for you. If not, no worries at all, just wanted to show you what I built."

---

## Quick Tips

- Keep it under 60 seconds
- Start with "thanks for replying"
- Reference their hook response (makes it feel personal)
- Mention you already built it (creates curiosity)
- End with a question
- Use their name once at the beginning
