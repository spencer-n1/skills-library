# Business Type: Home Services (HVAC, Electrician, Plumbing, etc.)

**Category:** Home Services
**Typical Client:** Local contractors, trade businesses
**Primary Goal:** Emergency calls, quote requests

---

## Recommended Pattern Stack

### Navigation
**Primary:** Simple sticky with phone prominent
- Phone number visible at all times
- "Call Now" urgency for emergencies
- Clean, no animation

**Alternative:** Split nav with emergency badge

### Hero
**Primary:** Split layout with form
- Headline left, contact form right
- Immediate lead capture
- "Get a Free Quote Today"

**Alternative:** Full image with phone CTA
- For portfolio-heavy businesses (landscaping, roofing)

### Sections (in order)

1. **Stats Bar** (`stats-bar.md`)
   - Years in business, 24/7 availability, satisfaction %
   - Critical for trades — establishes trust immediately

2. **Services** (`services-3-card.md`)
   - 3 main services
   - Examples: Installation, Repair, Maintenance

3. **Why Choose Us** (Home Services variant)
   - Licensed & Insured
   - 24/7 Emergency Service
   - Local & Family Owned
   - Satisfaction Guaranteed

4. **Reviews** (`reviews-carousel.md`)
   - Local customer testimonials
   - Google/Facebook review integration

5. **Service Area** (Optional)
   - Map of coverage area
   - List of cities served

### CTA
**Final Banner** (`final-banner.md`)
- Phone number HUGE
- "Call Now for 24/7 Service"
- Emergency availability emphasized

### Footer
**Standard Multi-Column** (`standard-multi-column.md`)
- Service areas listed
- License numbers
- Emergency contact

## Design Direction

**Colors:**
- Navy/Dark Blue (trust, professional): #1e3a5f, #1e40af
- Orange (urgency, action): #ea580c, #f97316
- Red (emergency): #dc2626
- Green (go, positive): #16a34a

**Typography:**
- Bold, readable sans-serif: Inter, Roboto, Open Sans
- Strong, masculine feel for trades

**Imagery:**
- Real team photos (not stock contractors)
- Before/after project photos
- Trucks/equipment (shows professionalism)
- Happy customers (with permission)

**Feel:**
- Reliable and trustworthy
- Available when needed (24/7 messaging)
- Local and community-focused
- Professional, not "shady contractor"

---

## Content Guidelines

**Headlines:**
- Focus on speed, reliability, availability
- "Fast, Reliable HVAC Service"
- "We're There When You Need Us"

**Trust Signals:**
- Licensed and insured (prominent)
- 24/7 emergency service
- Years in business
- Local/family owned
- Satisfaction guarantee

**Urgency Triggers:**
- "Same Day Service"
- "24/7 Emergency Line"
- "Free Estimates"
- Phone number everywhere

**Avoid:**
- Vague availability
- No license info
- Stock photos of random workers
- Hidden pricing (be upfront about estimates)

## Special Notes for Home Services

**Emergency Services:**
- Phone number must be click-to-call
- 24/7 availability should be prominent if true
- Emergency vs. non-emergency CTAs

**Trust is Everything:**
- Home services = strangers in homes
- License and insurance info critical
- Reviews are social proof
- Photos of actual team help

**Local SEO:**
- City names in content
- Service area map
- Local landmarks mentioned

---

## Prompt Assembly Notes

When building for home services:
1. Phone number in header, hero, and CTA sections
2. "Licensed & Insured" mentioned early
3. Emergency availability prominent if applicable
4. Real photography of team/trucks
5. Simple contact form (name, phone, service needed)
