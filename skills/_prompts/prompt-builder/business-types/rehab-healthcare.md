# Business Type: Rehab / Healthcare Facility

**Category:** Health/Wellness
**Typical Client:** Physical therapy, addiction rehab, chiropractic, med spa
**Primary Goal:** Appointment bookings, intake calls

---

## Recommended Pattern Stack

### Navigation
**Primary:** `blurred-background-gradient-text.md`
- Modern, approachable
- Gradient text adds energy/positivity
- Clean, contemporary feel

**Alternative:** Standard light nav for clinical feel

### Hero
**Primary:** `gradient-text-dark.md`
- Energetic, hopeful messaging
- Modern gradient text
- "Your Recovery Starts Here" vibe

**Alternative:** `full-clean-image.md` with facility photo
- Shows actual location/team
- Good for: Med spas, chiropractic (visual appeal)

### Sections (in order)

1. **Stats Bar** (`stats-bar.md`) — Optional
   - Patients helped, years of service, success rate
   - Builds immediate credibility

2. **Why Choose Us** (`why-choose-us-professional.md`)
   - Credentials, certifications, approach
   - Team photo or facility image

3. **Services** (`services-3-card.md`)
   - 3 main programs/services
   - Examples: Physical Therapy, Occupational Therapy, Speech Therapy

4. **Process** (`process-numbered-cards.md`)
   - 4 steps: Assessment → Treatment Plan → Active Care → Recovery
   - Demystifies the process

5. **Reviews** (`reviews-carousel.md`)
   - Patient testimonials
   - Before/after (if appropriate for service)

### CTA
**Final Banner** (`final-banner.md`)
- "Start Your Recovery Today"
- Insurance accepted mention (if true)
- Phone + form option

### Footer
**Standard Multi-Column** (`standard-multi-column.md`)
- Insurance info
- Hours of operation
- Contact methods

## Design Direction

**Colors:**
- Blues (trust, calm): #2563eb, #3b82f6
- Teals (healing, health): #0d9488, #14b8a6
- Soft greens (growth, renewal): #16a34a, #22c55e
- Or: Purple gradients for modern med spas

**Typography:**
- Clean sans-serif throughout: Inter, Roboto, Open Sans
- Rounded fonts for friendly feel (optional)

**Imagery:**
- Actual facility photos (not stock hospitals)
- Real staff (with permission)
- Patients in therapy (candid, hopeful)
- Modern equipment (clean, not scary)

**Feel:**
- Hopeful and positive
- Professional but warm
- Modern and clean (not clinical/cold)
- Supportive, not judgmental

---

## Content Guidelines

**Headlines:**
- Focus on recovery, hope, results
- "Reclaim Your Life" not "We Treat Addiction"
- "Move Better, Feel Better" not "Physical Therapy Services"

**Trust Signals:**
- Medical credentials
- Insurance accepted
- Licensed therapists
- Success stories
- Comfortable environment

**Avoid:**
- Clinical, cold language
- Shame-based messaging
- Stock photos of generic medical settings
- Overpromising results

## Special Notes for Rehab

**Sensitivity:**
- Addiction rehab requires extra care in messaging
- Emphasize privacy, confidentiality
- Focus on hope and possibility
- Avoid before/after images that could be triggering

**For Med Spas:**
- More visual focus (treatments, results)
- Can use more premium/luxury language
- Before/after galleries important

**For Physical Therapy:**
- Show active recovery (people moving, exercising)
- Emphasize return to activities
- Insurance coverage is key decision factor

---

## Prompt Assembly Notes

When building for rehab/healthcare:
1. Emphasize "welcoming environment" not "clinical facility"
2. Include insurance information prominently
3. Multiple contact methods (phone, form, chat)
4. HIPAA compliance mention (if collecting health info)
5. Mobile click-to-call essential
