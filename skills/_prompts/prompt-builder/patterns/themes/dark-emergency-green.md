# Dark Theme + Emergency Green Accent

**File:** `library/themes/dark-emergency-green.md`

---

## Source
Rapid Rooter Plumbing — rapid-flow-fix.base44.app (Updated GPT version)

---

## Best For
- Emergency services (plumbing, HVAC, electrical)
- 24/7 availability businesses
- Urgent response positioning
- Modern, bold brand feel

---

## Vibe
Urgent but professional. Dark backgrounds feel serious and immediate. Emergency green cuts through with high contrast, signaling "go" and "action." Clean, modern, no-nonsense.

---

## Color Palette

**Primary Background:** Near-black / charcoal
- `#0F1115` - Deepest background
- `#1A1F27` - Slightly lighter for sections/cards

**Accent Color:** Emergency Green
- `#19E37A` - Primary CTA color
- Use for buttons, highlights, urgent elements
- Glow effects on key CTAs (subtle)

**Text Colors:**
- `#F5F7FA` - Off-white for primary text
- `#A0A8B8` - Muted gray for secondary text

**Border/Subtle:**
- `#2A303A` - Subtle borders, dividers

---

## Visual Treatment

**Backgrounds:**
- Solid dark colors, minimal texture
- Subtle gradients between sections for depth
- Cards sit slightly above with subtle borders

**CTAs:**
- Emergency green buttons with dark text (or white text on green)
- Glow/pulse animation on primary emergency CTAs
- Phone number always prominent and high-contrast

**Cards:**
- Dark card backgrounds (#1A1F27)
- Subtle border (#2A303A)
- Emergency green accents on hover or icons

---

## When to Use
- Emergency services (plumbing, electrical, HVAC, locksmith)
- 24/7 availability businesses
- Speed/urgency is the main selling point
- Want modern, bold aesthetic
- Targeting younger/more tech-savvy audience

---

## When NOT to Use
- Luxury/premium positioning (green feels too urgent)
- Healthcare/medical (can feel alarming)
- Law firms (not professional enough)
- Home services where trust > speed (landscaping, etc.)
- Light/brands that need warmth or approachability
