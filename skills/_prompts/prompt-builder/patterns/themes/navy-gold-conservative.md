# Navy/Gold Conservative Theme

**File:** `library/themes/navy-gold-conservative.md`

---

## Source
Golden Gate Financial — golden-gate-wealth.base44.app

---

## Best For
- Financial advisors
- Retirement planning firms
- Estate planning attorneys
- Professional services requiring trust/conservatism
- Established businesses (20+ years positioning)

---

## Vibe
Established, trustworthy, conservative. Deep navy conveys stability and professionalism. Gold accents suggest premium service without flashiness. Clean, polished, reassuring - the visual equivalent of a well-tailored suit.

---

## Color Palette

**Primary Background:** White or off-white
- `#FFFFFF` or `#F8F9FA` - Clean, bright

**Secondary Background:** Soft gray for sections
- `#F5F5F7` - Subtle section differentiation

**Primary Color:** Deep Navy
- `#1A2B4A` - Headers, primary text, buttons
- `#0F1A2E` - Darker accents

**Accent Color:** Rich Gold
- `#C9A962` - Premium gold for highlights
- `#B8944F` - Darker gold for hover states
- Use sparingly - for trust badges, premium accents, key CTAs

**Text Colors:**
- `#1A2B4A` - Primary text (navy)
- `#4A5568` - Secondary/muted text
- `#FFFFFF` - Text on dark backgrounds

---

## Visual Treatment

**Backgrounds:**
- Predominantly white/off-white
- Soft gray sections for visual rhythm
- Deep navy for contrast sections (footer, CTA bands)

**Typography:**
- Serif or sophisticated sans-serif
- Clean, generous line spacing
- Professional, not trendy

**CTAs:**
- Navy buttons with gold accents
- Gold only for premium/emphasis moments
- Conservative hover states (subtle darkening)

**Cards:**
- White or light gray backgrounds
- Subtle shadows, not heavy
- Clean borders if used

**Trust Elements:**
- Credentials badges (CFP, CFA, etc.)
- Years in business prominent
- Professional headshots
- No flashy animations

---

## When to Use
- Financial advisors and planners
- Retirement planning services
- Estate planning attorneys
- Wealth management
- Professional services (20+ years experience positioning)
- Trust is the primary selling point
- Conservative clientele

---

## When NOT to Use
- Modern/startup positioning
- Urgent/emergency services (too calm)
- Younger demographics (feels too traditional)
- Creative industries (too conservative)
- Home services trades (disconnect)
