# Sage Green Healthcare Theme

**File:** `library/themes/sage-green-healthcare.md`

---

## Source
Evergreen Physical Therapy — evergreen-motion-flow.base44.app

---

## Best For
- Healthcare services
- Physical therapy
- Wellness / holistic health
- Medical practices
- Recovery/rehabilitation focus
- Healthcare in Pacific Northwest / Seattle area

---

## Vibe
Healing, calming, natural. Sage green evokes growth, renewal, and tranquility. Less clinical than pure white medical sites. Approachable and warm while maintaining professionalism.

---

## Color Palette

**Primary Background:** White
- `#FFFFFF` - Clean, bright

**Secondary Background:** Soft sage
- `#F0F5F2` - Very light sage for sections
- `#E8F0EA` - Slightly deeper for contrast

**Primary Color:** Sage green
- `#5B8C6F` - Primary sage (CTAs, headers)
- `#4A755C` - Darker sage for hover states
- `#7BA88C` - Lighter sage for accents

**Accent Color:** Deep forest (optional)
- `#2D4A3E` - For contrast text on light sage
- `#1F352B` - Darker for emphasis

**Text Colors:**
- `#2D4A3E` - Primary text (deep forest)
- `#5A6B6A` - Secondary/muted text
- `#FFFFFF` - Text on green backgrounds

---

## Visual Treatment

**Backgrounds:**
- Predominantly white
- Soft sage sections for visual rhythm
- Clean, airy, lots of breathing room
- Nature-inspired, not clinical

**Typography:**
- Clean sans-serif
- Friendly, approachable weight
- Clear hierarchy

**CTAs:**
- Sage green buttons
- White text on green
- Consistent throughout

**Cards:**
- White or light sage backgrounds
- Subtle shadows
- Icon-based trust points

## When to Use
- Physical therapy / rehabilitation
- Healthcare practices
- Wellness / holistic health
- Recovery-focused services
- Pacific Northwest markets
- Want approachable over clinical
- Nature/healing connection important

---

## When NOT to Use
- Urgent/emergency services (too calm)
- High-tech medical (feels too natural)
- Corporate healthcare (not clinical enough)
- Dark/luxury positioning
- Urban/modern aesthetic desired
