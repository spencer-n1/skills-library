# Lead Magnets

Create high-value lead magnets that attract ideal customers and convert visitors into leads.

## When to Use
- Building email list
- Generating qualified leads
- Segmenting audience by interest
- Nurturing prospects toward sale

## Don't Use When
- You can't deliver on the promise
- The lead magnet attracts wrong audience
- You don't have nurture sequence ready
- The value isn't high enough to warrant an email

## Lead Magnet Types

### Educational
**Best for:** B2B, complex products, trust building

**Formats:**
- **Ebooks/Guides:** Comprehensive topic coverage
- **Whitepapers:** Research, data, insights
- **Reports:** Industry benchmarks, trends
- **Webinars:** Live or recorded training
- **Courses:** Email or video lessons

**Success factors:**
- Deep, actionable content
- Professional design
- Clear outcome promise

### Tools & Resources
**Best for:** Utility, recurring use, high perceived value

**Formats:**
- **Templates:** Spreadsheets, docs, frameworks
- **Checklists:** Process steps, best practices
- **Calculators:** ROI, pricing, savings
- **Toolkits:** Resource collections
- **Swipe files:** Examples, copy, designs

**Success factors:**
- Immediate usability
- Saves time or effort
- Professional quality
- Easy to customize

### Interactive
**Best for:** Engagement, qualification, personalization

**Formats:**
- **Quizzes:** Assessment, personality, diagnostic
- **Assessments:** Score, grade, benchmark
- **Generators:** Names, ideas, copy
- **Graders:** Audit, score current state

**Success factors:**
- Personalized results
- Shareable outcomes
- Clear action based on results

### Access
**Best for:** Community, exclusive content

**Formats:**
- **Free trials:** Product access
- **Demos:** Guided product experience
- **Consultations:** Expert time
- **Community access:** Private groups
- **Discounts:** First purchase incentive

## Lead Magnet Criteria

**High-Converting Lead Magnets Are:**

1. **Specific** — Clear, narrow topic
   - ❌ "Marketing Guide"
   - ✅ "LinkedIn Ad Copy Templates for B2B SaaS"

2. **Actionable** — Can use immediately
   - Theory is fine, but include "how to"

3. **Valuable** — Worth paying for (in theory)
   - Would you charge $10-50 for this?

4. **Quick Win** — Fast gratification
   - Can they benefit in <1 hour?

5. **Relevant** — Aligns with your product
   - Leads to your paid offering naturally

## Creation Process

### 1. Audience Research
- What problems keep them up at night?
- What have they tried that failed?
- What quick win would excite them?
- Survey existing customers/leads

### 2. Topic Selection
**Formula:** [Specific audience] + [Specific problem] + [Quick solution]

**Examples:**
- "Freelance Designers: Price Your Projects Profitably"
- "SaaS Founders: Calculate Your LTV:CAC Ratio"

### 3. Content Development
- Outline key points
- Write comprehensive content
- Add examples and templates
- Include actionable steps

### 4. Design & Packaging
- Professional PDF design
- Branded cover page
- Clear table of contents
- Scannable formatting
- Call-to-action at the end

### 5. Landing Page
- Compelling headline
- Clear value proposition
- Preview/benefits list
- Simple form (email minimum)
- Social proof

### 6. Delivery & Follow-up
- Immediate email delivery
- Thank you page with next step
- Nurture sequence triggered
- Retargeting pixel for non-converters

## Landing Page Elements

**Above the Fold:**
- Headline with benefit
- Subheadline with specificity
- Hero image/mockup
- Email form + CTA button

**Below the Fold:**
- What's inside (bullet points)
- Who it's for
- Preview/screenshots
- Testimonials
- Author bio/credentials
- FAQ
- Final CTA

## Promotion Strategies

**Organic:**
- Blog content upgrades
- Social media promotion
- Guest posts with lead magnet CTA
- Podcast appearances
- YouTube descriptions

**Paid:**
- Facebook/Instagram lead ads
- LinkedIn sponsored content
- Google Ads (high intent keywords)
- Retargeting campaigns

**Partnerships:**
- Co-marketing with complementary brands
- Affiliate/referral programs
- Bundle with other lead magnets

## Metrics to Track

**Conversion:**
- Landing page conversion rate (target: 20-40%)
- Cost per lead (CPL)
- Lead quality (conversion to customer)

**Engagement:**
- Open rate of delivery email
- Download rate
- Consumption (if trackable)

**Business Impact:**
- Lead magnet attribution to pipeline
- Lead magnet attribution to revenue
- Time to conversion
